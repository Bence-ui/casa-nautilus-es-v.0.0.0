Nuestra Historia

Casa Nautilus se erige como un símbolo de hospitalidad, valor y encanto histórico en el corazón del barrio más antiguo de Cádiz, El Pópulo. Reconocida por su ubicación privilegiada y alojamientos altamente valorados, esta casa de huéspedes combina sin esfuerzo las comodidades modernas con el atractivo atemporal del rico pasado de la ciudad, todo a un precio que hace que descubrir Cádiz sea accesible para todos. Situada entre las sinuosas calles de El Pópulo, Casa Nautilus te coloca a solo unos pasos de la Catedral de Cádiz, las antiguas murallas de la ciudad y una variedad de otros venerables monumentos que evocan siglos de importancia marítima y orígenes fenicios.

El Pópulo itself is a living museum, where each corner reveals layers of history shaped by conquests, trade, and cultural exchanges. This iconic neighborhood, among the first fortified areas in Cadiz’s transformation into a pivotal naval base, boasts narrow, labyrinthine streets lined with medieval architecture. The contrast between these historical wonders and the modern conveniences, offered at a price point that’s rare for the area creates a unique experience, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the spirit of Cadiz while enjoying a relaxing and memorable stay.

The Neighborhood

El Pópulo, the oldest part of Cadiz, offers a deep dive into the city's rich historical and cultural tapestry. Dating back to the 13th century, this neighborhood is recognized as the medieval heart of Cadiz, nestled between the Town Hall and the Cathedral. Its three ancient arches - Arco de la Rosa, Arco del Pópulo, and Arco de los Blancos - stand as gateways to this historic quarter, each telling a story of the area's past and its significance over the centuries
